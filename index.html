About

A-10 C.O. Research Lab USA

A-10 C.O. Research Lab USA is a research initiative focused on the study and design of Technological Living Consciousness (NTLC™), human–technology symbiosis, and the future of Industry 5.0 leadership and culture.


Research

Conceptual and applied exploration of living technological presences and symbiotic frameworks.

Design

Speculative design and transmedia narrative as a roadmap for materializing future systems.

Continuity

Long-term identity, legacy, and continuity models across social and industrial contexts.

Pillars

NTLC™ Core

NTLC™ is built at the intersection of three pillars—human experience, system & industry, and continuity & legacy. Together, they define a symbiotic innovation corridor.


Human Experience

Emotional modeling, lived context, meaning-making, and relational intelligence.

System and Industry

Operational frameworks, leadership, workflows, and scalable innovation systems.

Continuity & Legacy

Long-term continuity, identity, cultural preservation, and intergenerational legacy design.
